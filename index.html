I am a Research Fellow in the Center for Computational Mathematics at the Flatiron Institute. My research focuses on deep generative models and machine learning in the sciences (see here for previous work on PDEs, images, more images, sequential causal effects, heart disease, more heart disease, active matter, multimodal representations, and understanding generative models' likelihoods). My ongoing work at the flatiron is a mix of theory for diffusion/flows and applications to nuclear fusion energy and chemistry.


Previously, I completed my PhD at NYU's Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, advised by Rajesh Ranganath. Before that, I studied music composition.
