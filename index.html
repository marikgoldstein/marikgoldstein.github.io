The Harvard natural-language processing group is a collection of researchers interested in computational models for human language. This broadly includes machine-learning and deep-learning methods, the study of artificial intelligence, and interestions between computer science and linguistics.

If you are at Harvard and interested in NLP/ML, come take one of our courses we offer in the area (CS181, CS182, CS187, CS287). We also hold a weekly reading group where we discuss new research and invite local speakers. If you are interested in a applying for a Ph.D or postdoc, please email srush@seas.harvard or stop by office hours Wed. 1-3 in MD 217.
